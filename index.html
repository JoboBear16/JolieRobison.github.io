Choclate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 1 hr 10 mins
  • Serves: 3 dozen
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking Soda 1/2 Teaspoon
Unsalted Butter 2 sticks (1 cup)
Granulated Sugar 1/2 cup
Light Brown Sugar 1 cup
Kosher Salt 1 Teaspoon
Vanilla Extract 2 Teaspoons
Eggs 2 Large Eggs
Chocolate Chips 12 Ounces

Step 1: Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees and Line baking sheets with parchment paper

Step 2: Combine flour with baking soda

Step 3: Beat butter and sugar separately

Step 4: Add salt, vanilla, and eggs

Step 5: Add flour mixture gradually

Step 6: Mix in chocolate chips

Step 7: Portion cookies and bake for 8 to 10 minutes

Banana Bread

  • Time: 1 hr 10 mins
  • Serves: 1 loaf
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1/2 cup (1 stick)
Sugar 1 cup
Flour 1 1/2 cups
Baking soda 1 Teaspoon
Kosher Salt 1 Teaspoon
Mashed Bananas 1 cup
Vanilla Extract 2 Teaspoons
Eggs 2 Large Eggs
Sour Cream 1/2 cup
Chopped Walnuts 1/2 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 Teaspoon

Step 1: Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees and buttera 9x5x3 inch loaf pan

Step 2: Cream butter and sugar together

Step 3: Beat in eggs gradually

Step 4: Whisk in dry ingredients

Step 5: Add nuts and transfer to prepared pan

Step 6: Bake about 1 hr 10 minutes

Pumpkin Muffin

  • Time: 1 hr
  • Serves: 12 muffins
Ingredients Quantity
Vegetable Oil 3/4 cup (1 stick)
Whole Wheat Flour 1 1/2 cup
All-purpose flour 1 1/2 cups
Baking Powder 2 Teaspoon
Baking Sodat 1/2 Teaspoon
Pumpkin Pie Spice 1 Teaspoon
Pumpkin Puree 2 cups
Eggs 3 Large Eggs
Plain Low-fat yogurt 1 cup
Turbinado Sugar 1 cup
Walnuts 1 1/2 cups

Step 1: Preheat Oven to 350 Degrees and brush 12 muffin tins with oil

Step 2: Whisk together flours, baking powder, pumpkin pie spice and baking soda

Step 3: Whisk oil, pumpkin puree, yogurt, eggs, and sugar then mix in dry ingredients and walnuts

Step 4: Divide Batter into muffin tins and sprinkle walnuts on top

Step 5: Bake for 35 to 40 muffins
